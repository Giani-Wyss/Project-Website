“Kleinstadt Deluxe” was released on 10 December 2021 and contains all eighteen songs from the recently released album “Kleinstadt”, plus three new songs.

After nearly two years without an album released Rin “Kleinstadt” on 29 October 2021. It contains eighteen songs and ten of them being new ones.

“Nimmerland” was Rin’s third album and was published on 6 December 2019. It contained nine new songs and five previously known songs.

The album "Planet Megatron", released on 17 August 2018, is Rin's shortest album with twelve songs. The first of these songs, "Dior 2001", is probably his best-known song.

“EROS”, released 1 September 2017, was Rin’s first Album. Fifteen all new songs appeared on it.
